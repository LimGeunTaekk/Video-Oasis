Removing Shortcuts Reveals the Real Challenge

Once shortcut-solvable samples are removed, benchmark accuracy drops sharply across state-of-the-art video-LLMs. On the remaining video-native challenges, most models perform only marginally above random chance. This reveals that high benchmark scores can mask a substantial gap in genuine spatio-temporal understanding.

Diagnostic framework

What is Video-Oasis?

Video-Oasis verifies whether each benchmark sample truly requires visual evidence and temporal context, while resolving ambiguity through human verification. These complementary diagnostics identify shortcut-solvable cases and isolate video-native challenges for genuine spatio-temporal understanding.

01 Visual

Determine whether the task truly requires grounded visual perception rather than language, audio, or text-based cues.

02 Temporal

Determine whether the answer depends on temporal order and context rather than isolated or unordered frames.

03 Ambiguity

Ensure that questions have reliable annotations, unique answers, and evidence anchored to the relevant video context.

Video-native challenges

What Challenges Remain After Filtering?

After filtering shortcut-solvable samples, V-Oasis distills five video-native challenges that require models to connect visual evidence across space and time, from fine-grained perception to long-range narrative understanding.

01

Fine-Grained Perception

Ground fine-grained visual recognition in how details evolve across space and time.

02

Spatial World Understanding

Synthesize fragmented, multi-view evidence across frames to infer 3D positions, geometry, and trajectories.

03

Temporal Dynamics & Tracking

Track objects, action sequences, and state transitions in their correct temporal order.

04

Causality & Logical Reasoning

Infer cause-and-effect relationships, physical rules, and unobserved intentions beyond visible events.

05

Global Narrative

Aggregate events across the full timeline, filtering irrelevant context to understand overarching plots and long-term semantics.

What Actually Drives Robust Video Understanding?

Using V-Oasis as a diagnostic testbed, we examine the hypotheses, evidence, and practical lessons behind stronger reasoning under strict spatio-temporal dependency.
